AMGEF: A Vision for a Brighter
Tomorrow

As we reflect on the journey of the Alpha Merit Group Educational Foundation, Inc. (AMGEF) since its inception in 1982, it becomes clear that our mission extends beyond individual events and programs. AMGEF embodies a vision for a brighter tomorrow, where every minority youth in the Houston Metropolitan area has access to quality education, mentorship, and opportunities for growth.

As we continue this journey, we invite you to be part of something greater. Join us in shaping the future, one scholarship, one mentorship, and one event at a time. AMGEF's story is not just about the past or present; it's about the limitless possibilities of a future where education transforms lives and communities. Together, let's paint a canvas of hope and opportunity for generations to come.
