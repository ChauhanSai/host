Celebrating 70 Years of Impact:
The Alpha Merit Toy Dance Gala

Get ready to don your black-tie attire and join us at the 70th Annual Alpha Merit Toy Dance Gala, hosted at the JW Marriott by The Galleria on February 10, 2024. This signature event has become a beacon of hope and support for minority youth in the Houston Metropolitan area. Our commitment to Leadership, Scholarship, and Service is reflected in the scholarships and development curricula we provide for young minds.


The gala serves as a platform to fundraise for our educational causes, bringing together individuals who share our vision for a brighter future. Attendees are invited to contribute to our annual toy drive by bringing an unwrapped toy of $15 value, spreading joy to children in need during the holiday season. Beyond the charitable aspect, the gala promises an enchanting evening filled with live entertainment, delicious dining, and an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals.


We invite you to share in the joy of giving and join us at the JW Marriott for a night of celebration, community support, and the spirit of making a difference. Secure your spot today and be a part of our ongoing efforts to uplift minority youth through education and opportunity.
