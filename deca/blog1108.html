Empowering Minority Youth Through
Education

In our commitment to empowering minority youth, the Alpha Merit Group Educational Foundation, Inc. (AMGEF) continues to make a significant impact in the Houston Metropolitan area since 1982. Our partnership with Big Brothers, Big Sisters of America has allowed us to advocate for one-to-one mentoring relationships that help children reach their full potential. Through social and educational activities, AMGEF raises awareness about BBBSA, actively recruits and retains male mentors, and participates in fundraising activities to support the organization's crucial initiatives.


Furthermore, our collaboration with Boy Scouts of America enables us to contribute to the development of local scouts. AMGEF provides merit badge counselors across various subject areas, supports local and state-wide merit badge workshops, and contributes to Eagle Scout projects. By engaging with youth in these meaningful ways, we aim to foster leadership skills, promote education, and positively influence the lives of minority youth in our community.


As we embark on this journey of education and empowerment, stay tuned for our upcoming events, including the 70th Annual Alpha Merit Toy Dance Gala, where we celebrate our achievements and continue to raise funds for educational programs.
