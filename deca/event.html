December 15

70th Annual Alpha Merit Toy Dance

Join us for dining, dancing and holiday cheer as we support minority youth in our community and raise funds for educational programs.


Leadership, Scholarship, and Service are the foundation of the Alpha Merit Group Educational Foundation, Incorporated (AMGEF). AMGEF provides scholarships and development curricula for minority youth throughout the Houston Metropolitan area. After a two-year hiatus, we are excited to announce and offer further details on the 70th Alpha Merit Toy Dance Gala.


Our signature black-tie gala has become one of the most anticipated events allowing us to fundraise to support our educational causes. The gala provides various charitable organizations within our historical communities with toys for the children they serve during this holiday season. We ask that you bring an unwrapped toy of $15 value as a contribution to our annual toy drive. We also ask that you have a festive spirit and partake in our planned spectacular live entertainment. We ask that you bring your dancing shoes and prepare to enjoy a celebratory occasion filled with food and spirits.


Finally, we ask that you share and encourage your friends and colleagues to sponsor a table or purchase an individual ticket. Again, this kind gesture supports our annual educational programming for minority youth in the Houston Metropolitan area.


We look forward to enjoying a beautiful night at the JW Marriott by The Galleria with you and yours.
