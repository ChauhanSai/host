AMGEF's Ripple Effect: Impacting
the Greater Houston Area

Beyond individual stories, the Alpha Merit Group Educational Foundation, Inc. (AMGEF) has created a ripple effect that resonates across the Greater Houston Area. Through our strategic partnerships with organizations like Big Brothers, Big Sisters of America, Boy Scouts of America, and Junior Achievement, AMGEF's influence extends far beyond the boundaries of our immediate community.


Imagine a community where mentorship is a driving force, where scouts engage in meaningful projects, and where young minds are equipped with essential business and economic principles. This is the vision AMGEF has been actively turning into reality for over four decades. By collaborating with like-minded entities and fostering relationships with diverse communities, AMGEF continues to make a lasting impact on the educational landscape of the Greater Houston Area.


As we celebrate our achievements, we also invite you to consider the collective impact we can make when communities unite for a common cause. Stay tuned for more updates on how AMGEF's initiatives are shaping the educational future of the entire Houston region.
